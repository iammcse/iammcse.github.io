The Two-Part Problem

Access requires solving both of these:

1

IP Geolocation

Gemini checks your IP address on every request. If your IP is from an unsupported region, you're blocked immediately.

2

Google Account Region

Your Google Account has a linked country profile (set via payment settings and terms of service). This must also appear to be from a supported country.

Explicitly blocked: China (mainland), Hong Kong, Russia, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Cuba, and other sanctioned regions face the strictest restrictions.

Step-by-Step Workaround

1

Use a Quality VPN or Proxy

Free VPNs and cheap data center IPs are almost universally detected and blocked. Choose carefully:

IP TypeDetection RiskVerdict
Data center IP (free VPNs) ~100% blocked Avoid
Static residential proxy ~15% detected Usable daily
4G / 5G mobile IP <5% detected Best for setup
  • Connect to a server in the US, UK, Japan, or Singapore
  • Keep the VPN on consistently — frequently switching IPs triggers Google's fraud detection
2

Change Your Google Account Region

  1. Go to payments.google.com → Settings
  2. Delete any payment methods tied to unsupported countries
  3. Under Billing & Shipping Address, change your country to a supported one (e.g., United States)
  4. Close or delete old payment profiles for unsupported countries
  5. Go to policies.google.com/terms — if it shows an unsupported country version, use the country association form to change it
3

Browser & Mobile Setup

Web Browser

  • Clear all Google cookies and cache, or use a fresh Incognito / Private window
  • Use a proxy extension like SwitchyOmega (Chrome) to route only Google traffic through the proxy
  • Try a different browser if issues persist

Android

  • Download the Gemini APK from APKMirror or APKPure (sideload)
  • In phone settings → Apps → Google / Gemini → disable Location permission
  • Use mobile data (4G / 5G) instead of Wi-Fi — this sometimes bypasses stricter checks

iOS

  • Switch your Apple ID region to US, then download from the App Store
  • Disable GPS / location for the Google app in Settings
4

Verify Account Settings

  • Go to myactivity.google.com/product/gemini and ensure "Gemini Apps Activity" is turned ON
  • Use a personal @gmail.com account (not Workspace or school)
  • Confirm the account's date of birth makes you 18 or older

Important Warnings

Use a separate Google account — don't risk your main account with region changes and proxies.
Don't frequently switch VPN nodes — Google flags this as suspicious behavior and may lock your account.
Turn off the VPN when not using Gemini — it slows normal browsing and some services may behave unexpectedly.
Account region changes have side effects — changing your Google Account's country can affect YouTube Premium, Play Store purchases, Google One, and other services tied to that account.
